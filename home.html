Welcome!

Hey! I’m Daniel Hill. I am currently attending Clovis West High School and the Center for Advanced Research and Education (CART). I will graduate in June, 2023, which scares me, but I’m ready for the challenge. At CART I am taking the Web Applications Development class. Through that class I have learned to make several websites with HTML and CSS, and I have CIW certification in JavaScript. I’m pretty much as big of a nerd as you can get. I like movies, games, D&D, and technology. One day I want to build a whole fantasy world, Tolkien style, to play D&D games in. I’m really proud of an interactive story project I did for CART. My partner and I wrote a huge story and coded all the JavaScript that allows it to function properly. Come check it out along with the rest of my work in my personal portfolio here.
